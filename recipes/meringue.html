  • Meringues
  • Meringues are a sweet and airy desert made from whipped egg whites and sugar. Its light and delicate texture coupled with its sweetness has made it a popular dessert around the world.

Prep Time: ~1hr 15 mins | Servings: 4-6 (sheet tray)

Directions

  1. Add egg whites, salt, and lemon juice to a bowl. Whisk until frothy
  2. Slowly wisk in sugar
  3. Whip until you get stiff peaks
  4. Transfer to piping bag and pipe onto parchment lined tray
  5. Bake at 200F for 1 hour
  6. Let cool, then serve

Ingredients

  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice or white
    • vinegar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup granulated or ultra-fine
    • sugar
