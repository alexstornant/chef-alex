Chef Alex
  • Roast Chicken
  • Roast chicken is a great and hearty dinner. It has golden-brown, crispy skin with juicy meat that falls off the bone. The aroma spices wafts through the air, enticing the senses. Each bite is a burst of savory goodness that makes roast chicken a timeless classic.

Prep Time: ~1 hour 30 mins | Servings: 4-6

Directions

  1. Clean and dry chicken with paper towels
  2. Cut lemon in quarters, onion in half and garlic in half
  3. Stuff cavity with above
  4. Drizzle olive oil over all of chicken
  5. Rub in seasonings
  6. Tie legs closed with twine if you have
  7. Roast at 400F for 15 mins per pound
  8. Temp should be around 180F in thickest part of breast
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes, then serve

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic bulb
  • 1 onion
  • 1-2 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
  • Italian seasoning, paprika
  • Any other spices you'd like*
  • Kitchen twine*

*optional
