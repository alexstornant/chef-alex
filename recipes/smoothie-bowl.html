  • Smoothie Bowl
  • Smoothie bowls are a delicious and nutritious breakfast or snack option that combines the refreshing taste of a smoothie with the satisfying texture of a bowl. Smoothie bowls offer a customizable and creative way to enjoy a nutrient-packed meal, providing a balance of flavors and textures in every spoonful.

Prep Time: 5-10mins | Servings: 1

Directions

  1. Put fruit, kefir, and honey into a blender
  2. If you've never tried kefir, it is kind of like yogurt but a little sour
  3. The fruit being frozen is important so the bowl will be thicker, the kefir helps as well
  4. You can remove/add any fruits
  5. Once done with blender, put in a bowl and add toppings like granola and/or berries*

Ingredients

  • Frozen strawberries
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Frozen bananas
  • Plain kefir
  • Honey
  • Granola*
  • Fresh Berries*

*optional
