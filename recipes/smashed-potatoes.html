  • Smashed Potatoes
  • These smashed potatoes are a very tasty side dish or snack! They're crispy on the outside, creamy in the middle, and packed with a savory flavor. With a side of a yogurt based dip, they are delicious.

Prep Time: ~45 mins | Servings: 2-3

Directions

  1. Boil a pot of water and salt, preheat oven to 450
  2. Add potatoes to boiling water, let boil until fork tender (~10-12 mins)
  3. Strain potatoes and let cool enough to handle
  4. Put into bowl with oil and season
  5. Transfer to baking sheet
  6. Use water glass and smash potatoes flat
  7. Put into over for 30 minutes
  8. For dip mix yogurt, lemon, onions, dill, and chives in bowl
  9. Serve potatoes with dip

Ingredients

  • 1 pound baby yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 tbsp fresh minced dill
  • 2 tbsp fresh sliced chives
