  • Pesto Chicken Sandwich
  • Pesto chicken sandwiches are a great lunch that brings together chicken with the vibrant flavors of pesto sauce. Nestled between your preferred bread, the sandwich is layered with fresh cheese, optionally tomato slices, and pesto. This combination creates a satisfying and savory sandwich, perfect for those seeking a quick and flavorful meal.

Prep Time: 20 mins | Servings: 1-2

Directions

  1. Slice chicken breast in half and pound until even
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Put olive oil in pan and cook chicken breast
  4. Toast bread of your choice
  5. Put melted cheese and pesto on the bread
  6. Once chicken done cooking, lay on the bread
  7. Add more pesto/cheese/tomatoes* if necessary
  8. Slice in half, if sharing, and serve

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • Choice of sandwich bread
  • Pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Choice of cheese
  • 1 Tomato*

*optional
