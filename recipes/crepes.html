Chef Alex
  • Nutella Crepes
  • These crepes are a delicious breakfast or dessert that's perfect for any occasion. The thin, light French-style pancakes can be filled with anything, but this recipe they are filled with Nutella. Topped with fresh berries, powdered sugar, and whipped cream give that perfect finishing touch.

Prep Time: ~15 minutes | Servings: 4-6 crepes

Directions

  1. Mix milk, eggs, butter, sugar, salt, and vanilla extract into large bowl. Whisk or blend
  2. Once mixed, slowly sift in flour and continue whisking/blending until smooth
  3. Spread small layer onto lightly buttered skillet
  4. Cook until golden brown, flipping ~1 minute
  5. Add nutella, then fold
  6. Serve with berries, powdered sugar, whipped cream, or anything else

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter, melted and slightly cooled
  • 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Tsp fine sea salt or table salt
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour, sifted if desired
  • Nutella as much as you like
  • Berries*
  • Whipped cream & powdered sugar*

*optional
