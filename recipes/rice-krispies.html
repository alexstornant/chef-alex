Prep Time: 10 minutes | Servings: 16

Directions

  1. Get large pot and melt butter
  2. Once melted, add vanilla extract
  3. Then, slowly add in marshmellows
  4. Once melted, add in cereal and mix
  5. Once mixed, transfer to baking sheet and let cool
  6. Top with frosting and/or sprinkles*
  7. Refrigirate for up to 5 days, or freeze for months

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Marshmellows
  • Rice Krispie Cereal
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Frosting*
  • Sprinkles*

*optional
