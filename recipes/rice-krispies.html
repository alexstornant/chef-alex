  • Rice Krispie Treats
  • Rice krispie treats are one of my favorite snacks. They're pretty easy and fun to make with only a couple ingredients. I've made them for tailgates, halloween parties, and for my family. You can also make them into different shapes and colors. Overall, a very easy and versatile treat.

Prep Time: ~15 minutes | Servings: 8-10 (one 9x13 tray)

Directions

  1. Get large pot and melt butter slowly until lightly browned
  2. Once melted, add vanilla extract
  3. Slowly add in marshmallows
  4. Leave some marshmallows behind if you want in final product
  5. Once melted, add in cereal and mix
  6. Once almost finished mixed, add in extra marshmallows if wanted
  7. Once mixed, transfer to baking sheet/pan and let cool
  8. Top with frosting and/or sprinkles*
  9. Refrigerate for up to 5 days, or freeze for months

Ingredients

  • 8 Tbsp Butter
  • 16 oz Marshmallows
  • 6 cups Rice Krispie Cereal
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Frosting*
  • Sprinkles*

*optional
