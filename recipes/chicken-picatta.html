  • Chicken Picatta
  • Chicken piccata is a classic Italian-American dish that features tender chicken cutlets coated in a tangy lemon-butter sauce with briny capers. It's a quick and easy weeknight meal that's sure to please everyone at the table.

Prep Time: 30 mins | Servings: 2

Directions

  1. Slice chicken breast in half and pound until even thickness
  2. Cover breast in seasoned (garlic powder, salt, pepper) flour
  3. Put oil into sauteing pan, let heat up
  4. Put in chicken breasts, and sear for 2-3 minutes per side
  5. Remove chicken and add shallot with small amount of oil
  6. Put zest and lemon juice into pan
  7. Add chicken stock, capers, and chicken breasts back to simmer
  8. Reduce heat and cook chicken 2-3 mins
  9. Remove chicken, and add butter to complete sauce
  10. Stir until smooth
  11. Serve sauce on chicken with potatoes or pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • All purpose flour
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 2-3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Shallot
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • Capers
  • 3 Tbsp butter
